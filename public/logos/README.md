# Company Logos

Place company logo images in this directory:

- `auvik.png` - Auvik Networks Inc. logo
- `sew-eurodrive.png` - SEW Eurodrive of Canada logo  
- `utoronto.png` - University of Toronto logo

Recommended size: 48x48px or larger (will be scaled to fit)
Formats: PNG, SVG, or JPG

If logos are not provided, a placeholder with the company's first letter will be displayed.
